- Used `tasteProfile.isLoaded` (not `topTasteTags.length === 0`) for the skeleton check on Explore — critical distinction per RESEARCH.md Pitfall 6: both loading AND empty profiles have zero tags.
- Did not duplicate `skeleton-line`/`skeleton-pulse` CSS in Explore — they were already defined for the results skeleton, reused correctly.
