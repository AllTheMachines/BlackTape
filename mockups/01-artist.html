About
Wikipedia

Stars of the Lid is an American ambient music duo from Austin, Texas, consisting of Adam Wiltzie and Brian McBride. The band formed in 1993 and is known for their expansive, orchestral drone compositions — dense, slowly evolving soundscapes built from strings, brass, piano, and electronics. Their 2007 double album And Their Refinement of the Decline is widely regarded as one of the defining statements in orchestral ambient music. The name is a reference to eyelids and the visual experience of closing one's eyes.
